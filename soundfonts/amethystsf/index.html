Amethyst Imperial Grand

The Amethyst Imperial Grand.

This is my currently latest soundfont project, on which I have been working on for over 2 months. It is a Yamaha C7 Grand Piano soundfont with samples based on the ones from the free piano plugin called “Piano One” by SoundMagic. This soundfont is specifically optimized and equalized to be used for Black MIDI playback. The piano consists of 88 FLAC samples with 387kbps bitrate, for the best sound quality. There are a total of 20 velocity layers for the standard piano preset for realism, as well as a preset with 128 velocity layers (created by Kep for his Steinway B-211 soundfont project). You can find a total of 33 SFZ presets, so you can get exactly the sound you are looking for! There are also some addons to the piano. One of them is a selection of string samples to give it a “dreamy” or “fantasy” feel. The other one is the mixture of samples from the XP-80 keyboard, as well as its fantasy sounds created by Kep.

You can download a copy of this soundfont using the button located below this text. The file is distributed via MEGA. Please read the Terms of Use for this soundfont, which can be found right under the download button.
