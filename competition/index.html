1st MBMSP International Blacking Technique Competition – Results

Big thanks to everyone who participated in the competition!

The results of the competition are finally here! With a total of 15 submissions, we think that it was a huge success. I would like to thank each and every one of the participants for spending time and effort on their MIDIs. I did not expect so many people to join to be honest! The 2nd MIBTC will take place (likely) in 2 years from now. Below you can find a list with all the scores with order from the highest to lowest score.

Each MIDI is scored on a scale from 1 to 300. There are 5 parts for every MIDI (which differ according to the type of the midi -Korean style or Visual based-) which are scored from 1-10 each, with 60 being the highest score for each part. The list is accordion type in order to be more compact.

The contestant with the most points will be offered to be a judge on the next MIBTC and to join the MBMS Productions Team!
